About Us

SpiceJet Virtual Airlines is a realism-focused Virtual Airline operating on Infinite Flight. We aim to provide professional flight operations, structured SOP procedures, and a disciplined aviation environment. Our pilots follow realistic cruise levels, proper ATC communication, and stable approach standards. We operate both domestic and international routes with detailed planning and scheduling.

About Infinite Flight

Infinite Flight is a global mobile flight simulator that allows pilots to fly realistic aircraft with live ATC and multiplayer servers. SpiceJet Virtual operates primarily on the Expert Server, maintaining high professionalism, proper communication standards, and safe operations at all times.
